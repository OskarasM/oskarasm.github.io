About Me

I am a Computer Games Developer in my first year of study with skills in programming games in C++ and C# through Visual Studio, Unity, and Unreal Engine 5. I have experience building 2D game frameworks, making 3D game prototypes and advancing existing games with developments such as new mechanics. Additionally, through my background in 3D modelling with Blender and graphic design with Adobe Illustrator, I can produce a variety of assets, from menus and logos to procedurally generated worlds with textures.

I am particularly interested in researching and advancing virtual reality and artificial intelligence technologies within games. In the future, I am looking to further explore and develop my skills in these areas to create large-scale and highly interactive games, such as VR MMO RPGs. My inspirations come from new emerging technologies, such as Unreal Engine 5, along with a range of media and games.

I enjoy leisure activities such as playing and coaching tennis, exploring games, and participating in online programming challenges. During my LTA tennis qualification course and my year of coaching experience, I have developed my communication and teaching skills, applying them in other areas such as tutoring. Exploring games and engaging with the industry gives me knowledge of the latest news. Programming challenges have helped with my proficiency in my Computer Games Development course at university.
