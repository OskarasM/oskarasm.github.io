The forefront of my work is complete in C++, creating engine tools, game mechanics and AI systems, though I have further professional proficiency in VFX with Unreal Engines Niagara system, 3D modelling with Blender and graphic design with Adobe Illustrator.

My future dream is to evolve the games industry through one or more groundbreaking technological developments in AI or mechanics, thus trying to get ahead by researching these fields in my free time.
